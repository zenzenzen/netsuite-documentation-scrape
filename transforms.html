Transform-Centric View

NetSuite Transform Workflows

This page separates transform workflows from the standard CRUD surface so you can scan the source-to-target routes faster. It is especially useful when you are tracing record creation chains like customer to salesOrder to invoice or salesOrder to itemFulfillment.
